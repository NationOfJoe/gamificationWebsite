Situation

    A well known company is interested in purchasing multiple services from PayTM, he requires that all of these services will run under one K8s cluster while every service has it’s own infrastructure requirements.
    PayTM top sales executive closed a huge deal with this client and promised his needs will be taken care of today!!
    To achieve this target, the management offered a prize to the team that will be able to complete this task in the shortest time and based on the customer requirements.
    Here are the requirements for the solution:

Solution Requirements

  • Must minimize costs through effective use of spot instances
  • Must be able to maintain capacity goals in an automated fashion
  • Must be reliable, as uptime is important for this critical application
  • Must provide a simplistic way to push an update/update an AMI to the cluster with no downtime
  • Cluster must be able to accommodate multiple different types of workloads
  • Ongoing management of the cluster must be an absolute minimum
